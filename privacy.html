UK Fuel Finder

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Location

UK Fuel Finder uses your approximate device location to show nearby petrol stations and their prices. Location is used only within the app to sort results by distance — it is not stored, linked to your identity, or shared with anyone.

Push Notifications

If you enable price alerts, your device's push notification token is stored on our server solely to deliver those alerts. It is not used for any other purpose and is not linked to any personal information.

Station & Price Data

Fuel price data is fetched from publicly available retailer feeds. No personal data is included in these requests.

Third-party Services

This app uses no third-party analytics or advertising services.
